What is FunkyFresh?

FunkyFresh is a community-based initiative that aims to minimize food waste on farms and in restaurants by combatting the consumer stigma of perfection. There is an enormous variety of fruits and vegetables that are thrown out simply because they are not perfectly shaped, sized, or uniform enough to meet the strict cosmetic standards of supermarkets. FunkyFresh is combatting this wasteful perception by creating alternate markets for "ugly" produce. Farmers typically struggle to sell "flawed" fruits and vegetables because supermarkets and food vendors only sell perfect looking produce. On the contrary, food establishments seek fresh and healthy produce that they can transform into delicious dishes. FunkyFresh is redefining food waste by delivering produce from farm to table and by returning restaurants' composted materials back to farms.
